A Dream Worth Living

Finding Strength in the Depths of Struggle Along the Continental Divide

An inspiring account of the 2014 Tour Divide race - a grueling 2,700-mile mountain bike journey from Canada to Mexico along the Continental Divide. This book chronicles not just the physical challenges of climbing mountain after mountain, but the mental and emotional transformation that occurs when we push beyond what we thought possible.

"This is why we ride the Tour Divide. In the span of a few hours, you can go from the brink of exhaustion in the worst possible conditions to an explosion of sunshine, amazing people, and breathtaking scenery."

Starting on Friday the 13th under a full moon and falling rain, this journey became more than just a race‚Äîit became a testament to human resilience, the power of community, and the extraordinary adventures that await when ordinary people refuse to accept ordinary limits.
